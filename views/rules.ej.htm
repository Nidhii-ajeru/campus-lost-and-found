Rules & Regulations

Things Remember before submitting

Section 1: Reporting Lost Items

  1. Report lost items to the campus security office immediately.
  2. Provide accurate information about the lost item's description, location, and time.
  3. Keep track of the reference number provided by the security office for follow-up.

Section 2: Checking Found Items

  1. Check the campus lost and found repository periodically for any items matching your lost belongings.
  2. Provide necessary identification and proof of ownership when claiming a found item.
  3. Be aware of any deadlines for claiming found items to avoid disposal.

Section 3: Found Item Handling

  1. Handle found items with care and integrity.
  2. Ensure found items are securely stored and documented to facilitate return to their rightful owners.
  3. Report any suspicious or unclaimed items to the campus security office.
